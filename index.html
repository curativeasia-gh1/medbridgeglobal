Core Services

End-to-End Market-Entry Capabilities

From regulatory strategy through to final delivery — Medbridge covers the complete operational pathway healthcare manufacturers need to enter and sustain market access in Asia.

Regulatory Market Entry

Device classification, regulatory pathway planning, registration submissions and post-market compliance support across all four markets.

Learn more →

In-Market Representation

Local authorised representation, Local Responsible Person coordination and regulatory-record management — using the correct legal designation in each country.

Learn more →

Licence Holding

Registration and licence structures that protect manufacturer flexibility — where permitted under local regulations. Distributor-independent where possible.

Learn more →

Importer of Record

Compliant importation without establishing full local infrastructure — covering import permits, customs documentation and shipment traceability.

Learn more →

Healthcare 3PL

Specialised storage and order fulfilment for regulated healthcare products — lot tracking, expiry management, pick-and-pack and consignment support.

Learn more →

Last-Mile Delivery

Scheduled delivery to hospitals, clinics and laboratories — appointment-based, with proof of delivery and status reporting as standard.
