Healthcare Specialities

Medical Device and Healthcare Specialities

Medbridge is not limited to a single product category. We support a wide range of medical device and healthcare product types across all supported markets. Only categories that Medbridge is legally, technically and operationally prepared to support are promoted as active capabilities.

🏥

Speciality 01

General Medical Devices

Medical devices used across hospital wards, outpatient clinics, rehabilitation centres and home healthcare settings. Medbridge supports manufacturers of general hospital equipment, diagnostic devices, surgical instruments and healthcare consumables across all four markets.

Speciality 02

Ophthalmology

Ophthalmic devices and surgical systems including diagnostic equipment, intraocular lenses, surgical instruments and clinic consumables. The region's growing ageing population and medical tourism sector support strong demand for ophthalmic products across all four markets.

🦷

Speciality 03

Dentistry & Maxillofacial Surgery

Dental imaging systems, digital dentistry platforms, intraoral and facial scanners, orthodontic products, oral surgery instruments and dental consumables. The region's growing dental and aesthetic surgery market makes this an important speciality across multiple Medbridge markets.

Product Categories

Dental imaging Digital-dentistry systems Intraoral scanners Facial scanners Orthodontic products Surgical instruments Dental consumables Oral surgery products

Product Categories

Speciality 04

Medical Aesthetics & Dermatology

Energy-based medical devices, skin-imaging systems, injectable products, professional skincare and clinic consumables. Medical aesthetics is a significant and growing category across Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia and Thailand, with strong demand from both clinic and hospital channels.

🦴

Speciality 05

Orthopaedics & Rehabilitation

Orthopaedic implants and instruments, bracing and supports, mobility aids, rehabilitation equipment and joint-care products. Surgical delivery coordination and consignment-stock management are particularly important for orthopaedic products — supported by Medbridge's last-mile logistics capabilities.

Product Categories

Orthopaedic devices Bracing and supports Mobility products Rehabilitation equipment Surgical instruments Joint-care products

Product Categories

In-vitro diagnostic devices Point-of-care systems Laboratory equipment Diagnostic consumables Sample-handling products

Speciality 06

Diagnostics & Laboratory

In-vitro diagnostic devices, point-of-care systems, laboratory equipment and diagnostic consumables. IVD products carry specific regulatory obligations in each market, including separate registration or listing requirements. Medbridge manages the IVD registration pathway and traceability requirements applicable in each country.
