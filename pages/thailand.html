Market Overview

Thailand Healthcare Market

Thailand is a significant healthcare and medical tourism market with a strong private hospital sector and growing domestic healthcare demand. Medical devices are regulated by the Thai Food and Drug Administration (Thai FDA) under the Medical Device Act. Medbridge supports manufacturers with Thai FDA market entry through local regulatory and logistics execution.

The Thai FDA regulates medical devices under the Medical Device Act B.E. 2551. A licensed local representative and import licence holder are required. Medbridge coordinates local representation, import permit management and Thai FDA submission support.
