Market Overview

Singapore Healthcare Market

Singapore is a highly regulated regional healthcare centre with one of Asia's most rigorous medical device frameworks. The market offers access to both public and private healthcare institutions with high standards for product quality and traceability. Medbridge supports manufacturers with HSA registration support, licence holding, IOR and regulated healthcare logistics.

Singapore's medical device framework is administered by the Health Sciences Authority (HSA) under the Health Products Act. Products are classified into four risk classes (A, B, C, D), with registration requirements varying by class. Medbridge supports manufacturers with classification advice, registration submissions, amendment management and ongoing post-market obligations.
