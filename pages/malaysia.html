Malaysia Healthcare Market

Malaysia offers access to a substantial healthcare market across both public Ministry of Health channels and a growing private hospital and clinic sector. Medical devices are regulated by the Medical Device Authority (MDA) under the Medical Device Act 2012. Medbridge supports manufacturers with MDA registration, local authorised representation, establishment licensing and nationwide logistics coordination.

The Medical Device Authority (MDA) regulates all medical devices in Malaysia under the Medical Device Act 2012. Products must be registered with the MDA and manufacturers must appoint a local authorised representative. Medbridge coordinates MDA registration, authorised representation and establishment licensing requirements.
