About Medbridge

Hong Kong-Headquartered Healthcare Market Access Across Asia

One accountable regional partner for regulatory access, representation, importation and delivery across four key Asian markets.

Who We Are

Built for Healthcare Manufacturers Entering Asia

Medbridge is a healthcare market-access and logistics company headquartered in Hong Kong. We support medical device and healthcare manufacturers across Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia and Thailand with regulatory registration, in-market representation, licence holding, importation support, healthcare 3PL, inventory management, last-mile delivery and selected commercial market-access services.

By combining regulatory services, local representation, importation and healthcare logistics, Medbridge enables manufacturers to build regional market access without establishing a complete operating infrastructure in every country.

How We Work

Operating Principles

Five principles guide how Medbridge structures its services and relationships with clients.

Regional

Multiple markets coordinated through one regional account and reporting structure. Manufacturers deal with one team, one contract and one consolidated view.

Local

Each market is managed according to its own regulatory, importation and logistics requirements — with qualified local teams and approved partners.

Transparent

Clear scopes, timelines, responsibilities and progress reporting. No hidden fees, no vague commitments. Fees and responsibilities documented and agreed upfront.

Commercially Practical

Regulatory and logistics decisions are aligned with long-term market objectives — not theoretical compliance frameworks that don't reflect commercial realities.

Healthcare-Focused

Quality controls, traceability and delivery processes are designed around regulated healthcare products — not repurposed from general logistics.

Scalable

The model begins asset-light and adds infrastructure only where volume and service economics justify it — protecting clients from fixed costs before scale.

Operating Model

Regional Coordination. Local Execution.

Hong Kong headquarters serves as the central control centre. Country-level activities are delivered through qualified local teams and approved partners.
